April Savoy

420 University Blvd United States · Indianapolis, IN 46202 · (317) 274-5555 · asavoy@iu.edu

I'm an Assistant Professor of Computer Information and Technology at IUPUI campus. I am a Core Investigator in the VA HSR&D Center for Health Information and Communication and Director of the Human-Computer Interaction & Simulation Lab at the Richard L. Roudebush Veteran Affairs Medical Center in Indianapolis. As appointed Regenstrief Research Scientist in 2017, my research interests include the implementation of user-centered design to develop, evaluate, and integrate health information technologies that will increase efficiency of clinicians’ workflow, enhance care coordination, and improve the quality of healthcare delivery.
